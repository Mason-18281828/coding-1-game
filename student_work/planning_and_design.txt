Part I: Planning & Design (15 pts)

Game Idea:
Move around a randomly generated maze to collect things until you have enough to spawn a portal room

Game Rules:
Collect lots of gems
Get to a portal room
Player actions: move around, press e to collect chest
there will be walls, enemies, chests, gems (in chests), speed upgrades (in different chests), and decoration
win condition: go through a portal
lose condition: get hit enough times or hit by the boss.
scoring: how few rooms and few hearts lost times the difficutly and the boss

6 by 7 (3 x 3 corners always filled in, 3x3 to be possible openings, 3x3 middle, plus a bottom row for stats)

Major Game Elements:
collect stuff. dodge enemies. go through portal. be speedy.

Gameplay Process:

first create the background(s), then the enemies, player, gems, chests, boots, hearts, and plants. icons: 
next create pathing for the monsters and rng systems for the chests, boots, plants and monsters
then create a way to change rng systems and loading screens
finally, create the boss mode
